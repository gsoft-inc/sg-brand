| 4     | 1.5rem (24px)   | f4    |

| 7     | 1rem (16px)     | f7    |

## Line Heights

There is 6 line heights available to you, these are based on a scale principle.

## Line Height Scale

Line heights are based on a scale of 6

| Scale | Value        | Class |
| :---- | :----------- | :---- |
| 1     | 1            | lh1   |
| 2     | 1.2          | lh2   |
| 3     | 1.25         | lh3   |
| 4     | 1.33333      | lh4   |
| 5     | 1.4285714286 | lh5   |
| 6     | 1.5          | lh6   |
