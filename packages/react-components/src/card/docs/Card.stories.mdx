## Usage

### Default

A card must have an heading and a content.

<Preview style={{ "width": "300px" }}>
    <Story name="default">
        <Card>
            <Heading>NASA Headquarters</Heading>
            <Content>
                300 E. Street SW, Suite 5R30<br/>
            Washington, DC 20546<br/>
            (202) 358-0001 (Office)<br/>
            (202) 358-4338 (Fax)
            </Content>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Image

A card can have an [image](?path=/story/chromatic-image--default):

<Preview scope={{ NasaHeadquarters }} style={{ "width": "300px" }}>
    <Story name="image">
        <Card>
            <Image src={NasaHeadquarters} alt="Appolo 11 Banner" />
            <Heading>Nasa Headquarters</Heading>
            <Content>
                300 E. Street SW, Suite 5R30<br/>
            Washington, DC 20546<br/>
            (202) 358-0001 (Office)<br/>
            (202) 358-4338 (Fax)
            </Content>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>

Or a centered [illustration](?path=/docs/illustration--horizontal) with a background color:

<Preview scope={{ Nasa }} style={{ "width": "300px" }}>
    <Story name="illustration">
        <Card >
            <Illustration color="marine-100">
                <Image src={Nasa} alt="NASA" width="100px" />
            </Illustration>
            <Heading>Nasa Headquarters</Heading>
            <Content>
            300 E. Street SW, Suite 5R30<br/>
            Washington, DC 20546<br/>
            (202) 358-0001 (Office)<br/>
            (202) 358-4338 (Fax)
            </Content>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Header

A card can have an header.

<Preview style={{ "width": "300px" }}>
    <Story name="header">
        <Card>
            <Heading>Nasa Headquarters</Heading>
            <Header>No unexpected visitors allowed.</Header>
            <Content>
            300 E. Street SW, Suite 5R30<br/>
            Washington, DC 20546<br/>
            (202) 358-0001 (Office)<br/>
            (202) 358-4338 (Fax)
            </Content>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Buttons

A card can have a single [button](?path=/docs/button--default-story):

<Preview style={{ "width": "300px" }}>
    <Story name="button">
        <Card>
            <Heading>Nasa Headquarters</Heading>
            <Header>No unexpected visitors allowed.</Header>
            <Content>
            300 E. Street SW, Suite 5R30<br/>
            Washington, DC 20546<br/>
            (202) 358-0001 (Office)<br/>
            (202) 358-4338 (Fax)
            </Content>
            <Button>Plan a visit</Button>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>

Or a [group of button](?path=/docs/button--default-story#button-group):

<Preview style={{ "width": "300px" }}>
    <Story name="buttons">
        <Card >
            <Heading>Nasa Headquarters</Heading>
            <Content>
                <Paragraph>
            300 E. Street SW, Suite 5R30<br/>
            Washington, DC 20546<br/>
            (202) 358-0001 (Office)<br/>
            (202) 358-4338 (Fax)<br/>
                </Paragraph>
                <Paragraph>
                    <em>Any trespassers are going to be sent in space.</em>
                </Paragraph>
            </Content>
            <ButtonGroup>
                <Button variant="outline">Cancel a Booking</Button>
                <Button>Plan a Visit</Button>
            </ButtonGroup>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Orientation

A card can render horizontally.

<Preview scope={{ Nasa }}>
    <Story name="horizontal">
        <Card orientation="horizontal">
            <Illustration color="marine-100">
                <Image src={Nasa} alt="NASA" width="100px" />
            </Illustration>
            <Heading>Nasa</Heading>
            <Content>
                <Paragraph>
                    300 E. Street SW, Suite 5R30<br/>
                    Washington, DC 20546<br/>
                    (202) 358-0001 (Office)<br/>
                    (202) 358-4338 (Fax)
                </Paragraph>
                <Paragraph>
                    <em>Please note that we are moving from December 12th to December 23rd.</em>
                </Paragraph>
            </Content>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Tab

A card can render any components like Tabs.

<Preview>
    <Story name="tab">
        <Card>
            <Heading>Nasa</Heading>
            <Content>
                <Tabs aria-label="NASA">
                    <Item key="vision">
                        <Header>Vision</Header>
                        <Content>We reach for new heights and reveal the unknown for the benefit of humankind</Content>
                    </Item>
                    <Item key="mission">
                        <Header>Mission</Header>
                        <Content>Drive advances in science, technology, aeronautics, and space exploration to enhance knowledge, education, innovation, economic vitality and stewardship of Earth</Content>
                    </Item>
                </Tabs>
            </Content>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Fluid

A card can take the width of its container.

<Preview>
    <Story name="fluid">
        <Card fluid>
            <Heading>Nasa</Heading>
            <Content>The National Aeronautics and Space Administration is an independent agency of the U.S. federal government responsible for the civilian space program, as well as aeronautics and space research.</Content>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Max Content

A card can adapt to the width of its content.

<Preview>
    <Story name="max-content">
        <Card>
            <Heading>Apollo 11</Heading>
            <Content>
                <Paragraph>
                    <strong>    Release Date</strong><br/>
                    <em>March 1, 2019</em>
                </Paragraph>
                <Paragraph>
                    The personal and professional lives of Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin and Michael Collins prior to their July 1969 moon mission.
                </Paragraph>
            </Content>
        </Card>
    </Story>
</Preview>
