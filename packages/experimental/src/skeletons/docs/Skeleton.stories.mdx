# Skeleton

<Tagline>Skeleton are used to provide a low fidelity representation of content before it appears on the page, and improves load times perceived by users.</Tagline>

### TextSkeleton

TextSkeleton already has a `height` style set, so you don't need to specify it yourself.

<Preview>
    <Story name="text-skeleton">
        <Stack>
            <Skeleton fluid>
                <Text size="sm">allo</Text>
            </Skeleton>
            <Skeleton fluid>
                <Text size="sm">allo</Text>
            </Skeleton>
            <Skeleton fluid>
                <Text>allo</Text>
            </Skeleton>
            <Skeleton fluid>
                <Text size="lg">allo</Text>
            </Skeleton>
            <Skeleton fluid>
                <Heading size="lg">allo</Heading>
            </Skeleton>
        </Stack>
    </Story>
</Preview>
