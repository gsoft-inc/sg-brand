## Font Size Scale

Font sizes are based on a scale of 9

| :---- | :-------------- | :---- |
| 1     | 3rem (48px)     | f1    |
| 2     | 2.5rem (40px)   | f2    |
| 3     | 2rem (32px)     | f3    |
| 4     | 1.5rem (24px)   | f4    |
| 5     | 1.25rem (20px)  | f5    |

| 7     | 1rem (16px)     | f7    |
