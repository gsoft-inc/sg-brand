## Rotation

To have an icon, e.g. a down arrow, use Tachyons rotate className

    <IconName className="rotate-90" />
    <IconName className="rotate-180" />
    <IconName className="rotate-270" />

<ArrowOutlineIcon className="w4 h4 mr5" />

<ArrowOutlineIcon className="w4 h4 mr5 rotate-180" />

<ArrowOutlineIcon className="w4 h4 mr5 rotate-90" />

<ArrowOutlineIcon className="w4 h4 rotate-270" />
