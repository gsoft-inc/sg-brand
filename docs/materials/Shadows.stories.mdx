CSS shadows are made using a system of 6 layers in order to provide silky smooth shadows. The shadows values can be previewed and altered on [shadows.brumm.af](https://shadows.brumm.af/).

#### Shadow 100

- Layers of shadows: 6
- Final transparency: 0.08
- Final vertical distance: 2
- Final blur strength: 10

#### Shadow 200

- Layers of shadows: 6
- Final transparency: 0.07
- Final vertical distance: 7
- Final blur strength: 20

#### Shadow 300

- Layers of shadows: 6
- Final transparency: 0.06
- Final vertical distance: 10
- Final blur strength: 40

#### Shadow 400

- Layers of shadows: 6
- Final transparency: 0.08
- Final vertical distance: 25
- Final blur strength: 80
